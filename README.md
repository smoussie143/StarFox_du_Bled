# StarFox du Bled

You want the last remake of StarFox on the Nintendo Switch 2 but you're 
completely broke? Then let me introduce you to StarFox du Bled! It's a 
free game developed in just a few days by a passionate teenager who's bad 
at programming. Journey through an intergalactic universe full of tiny 
stars and monsters to blast away. The future of shoot'em up is just one 
click away!

## Controls

- ↑↓→← : Move the spaceship
- C : Start the game
- X : Shoot

## What does "Bled" mean?

It's a French/Arabic slang term for one's family homeland (usually North 
Africa). In this context, it's used humorously to mean something homemade 
or budget-friendly.
